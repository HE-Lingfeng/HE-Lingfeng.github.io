Multimodal Reasoning / Generative AI / AI Agents

Lingfeng He

I am an M.S. student in Computer Science and Technology at Nanjing University of Science and Technology. My current interests center on the reasoning capabilities of multimodal large language models, image editing and generation, and AI agents that can plan, use tools, and act in open-ended environments.

Selected Work Email GitHub

Direction

Building models that reason, create, and act with intent.

I am interested in how intelligent systems move beyond pattern recognition: how they reason over multimodal context, translate abstract intent into visual creation, and coordinate tool use across multi-step tasks.
